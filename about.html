About Hafiz Asad Maqbool

Dedicated to spreading the light of Quranic knowledge

Assalam u Alaikum

My name is Hafiz Asad Maqbool, and I am honored to serve as an online Quran teacher, helping Muslims around the world connect with the Holy Quran. Teaching the Quran is not just my profession—it is my passion and my purpose.

I completed my Hifz-ul-Quran at a young age from a renowned Islamic seminary in Pakistan. Since then, I have dedicated my life to Islamic education, continuously improving my knowledge and teaching skills to provide the best learning experience for my students.

Over the years, I have had the privilege of teaching hundreds of students from diverse backgrounds and age groups. From young children taking their first steps in Quranic learning to adults seeking to deepen their understanding of Islam, I tailor my teaching approach to meet each student's unique needs.

My goal is simple: to make Quranic education accessible, effective, and spiritually enriching for everyone, regardless of where they are in the world.

Qualifications & Certifications

Hafiz-e-Quran

Completed memorization of the entire Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and pronunciation under the guidance of qualified scholars.

Ijazah in Quranic Recitation

Received Ijazah (certification) in Quranic recitation with authentic chain of narration (Sanad) from recognized Islamic institutions.

Islamic Studies Degree

Bachelor's degree in Islamic Studies with specialization in Quranic Sciences, Tajweed, and Tafseer from a reputable Islamic university.

8+ Years Teaching Experience

Extensive experience teaching Quran online to students from USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and many other countries worldwide.

My Teaching Style & Methodology

Student-Centered Approach

I believe every student is unique with different learning paces and styles. My teaching is personalized to match each student's needs, ensuring they feel comfortable and confident throughout their learning journey.

Interactive & Engaging

I use modern teaching tools, visual aids, and interactive exercises to make classes engaging and effective. Learning should be enjoyable, not burdensome.

Patience & Encouragement

I understand that learning the Quran can be challenging, especially for beginners. I am patient, supportive, and always encourage my students to do their best without pressure.

Holistic Islamic Education

Beyond just recitation, I incorporate Islamic values, character building, and spiritual development into my lessons, helping students develop a deeper connection with Allah and the Quran.

My Vision

To create a global community of Quran learners who not only recite the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed but also understand, implement, and live by its teachings in their daily lives. I envision a world where Quranic education is accessible to every Muslim, regardless of geographical boundaries.

My Mission

To provide high-quality, personalized Quranic education through one-on-one online classes that are flexible, affordable, and effective. My mission is to help every student develop a strong, lifelong relationship with the Quran through proper recitation, understanding, and spiritual connection.
